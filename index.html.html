Frequently asked questions

How is this different from ChatGPT?
ChatGPT is a general tool that needs good prompting and produces generic, robotic-sounding output. MailCraft AI is built specifically for professional and client emails — fill 3 fields, pick a tone, and get a polished email that sounds human. No prompt engineering required. It's the difference between a hammer and a nail gun.
Will clients know my email was written by AI?
No — and that's the whole point. MailCraft adapts to your chosen tone and style. Most users edit less than 5% of the output before sending. Emails read naturally, not like a chatbot.
Is my email content safe and private?
Yes. Your content is processed in real time and deleted within 24 hours. We never store, share, or train any AI model on your emails. Period.
What if I run out of emails?
The free plan covers casual use (5/month). If you're freelancing or running a business seriously, the Regular plan at $6.95 pays for itself with one won client. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime — no phone calls, no emails.
Can I cancel anytime? Is there a refund?
Yes — one-click cancel from your dashboard. And if you're not happy within the first 14 days, we refund 100%. No questions, no forms.
Does it work for non-English emails?
Absolutely. MailCraft supports 30+ languages including English, Urdu, Arabic, Hindi, Spanish, French, German, Chinese, Portuguese and more. Write to clients in their language, without translation headaches.
Who is MailCraft AI for?
Freelancers sending Upwork/Fiverr proposals, consultants handling client replies, founders doing cold outreach, job seekers writing to recruiters, and any professional who sends important emails and can't afford them to sound generic.
