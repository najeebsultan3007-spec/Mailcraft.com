FAQ

Questions answered.

Why not just use ChatGPT for free?
ChatGPT is brilliant — but using it for email takes 5–10 minutes per email. You need to write a detailed prompt, get a generic result, edit it, iterate. MailsCraft is purpose-built: 40+ ready-made email templates, a tone selector tuned for email psychology, one-click output. It's the difference between using a Swiss army knife and a proper scalpel. Specialists beat generalists for specific jobs.
Will recipients know it was AI-written?
No. MailsCraft is specifically trained to avoid AI "tells" — filler phrases, robotic transitions, generic openers. When you add personalization (name, company, context), the output reads entirely human. Thousands of users send MailsCraft emails daily with no one ever knowing. The Assertive and Persuasive tones in particular read very naturally.
What if I don't like the email it generates?
Regenerate with one click — each generation is different. You can adjust the tone, add more context, or tweak the output manually. On Pro and Business plans you can also add custom instructions that persist across all your emails, so MailsCraft learns your style over time.
Is my data private?
Yes. Your prompts and generated emails are not used to train AI models. We don't sell or share your data. Your email history (Pro+) is encrypted and only accessible to you. We take privacy seriously — especially because your emails contain sensitive business context.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes, no questions asked. Cancel from your account settings — your plan stays active until the end of the billing period. No contracts, no cancellation fees. We want you to stay because MailsCraft works, not because we trapped you.
Does it work for languages other than English?
Yes. MailsCraft supports 20+ languages including Urdu, Arabic, Spanish, French, German, Hindi, Portuguese, and more. Select your language and the entire email — including the subject line — is generated natively, not translated.
