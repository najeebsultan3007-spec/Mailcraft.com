Frequently asked questions

What is MailCraft AI?
MailCraft AI is an AI-powered email writing platform that helps professionals write better emails faster. You describe your goal, pick your tone and style, and our AI generates a complete, polished email in seconds.
Is my email content private?
Absolutely. Your email content is processed in real time and never stored on our servers. We don't read, save, or use your personal email content for any purpose.
How many languages are supported?
MailCraft AI supports 30+ languages including English, Urdu, Arabic, French, German, Spanish, Chinese, Hindi, Portuguese, Japanese, and many more.
What happens when I reach my free limit?
Once you've used your 5 free emails, you can upgrade to any paid plan. Plans start at just $6.95/month and can be cancelled anytime.
Does it work with Gmail or Outlook?
Yes — our Chrome extension integrates directly into Gmail. For Outlook, simply copy the generated email and paste it in. Direct Outlook integration is coming soon.
Will emails sound like AI wrote them?
No — MailCraft AI adapts to your specified tone, style, and context. The emails read naturally and authentically. Our users regularly report that recipients can't tell the difference.
