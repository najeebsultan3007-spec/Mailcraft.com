Frequently asked questions

What is MailCraft AI?
MailCraft AI is an AI-powered email writing platform that helps professionals write better emails faster. You describe your goal, pick your tone and style, and our AI generates a complete, polished email in seconds. It's used by 320,000+ professionals across real estate, export, marketing, HR, and more.
Is my email content private and secure?
Absolutely. Your email content is processed in real time and never stored on our servers. We don't read, save, or use your personal email content for any purpose.
How many languages are supported?
MailCraft AI supports 30+ languages including English, Urdu, Arabic, French, German, Spanish, Chinese, Hindi, Portuguese, Japanese, and many more.
What happens when I reach my free plan limit?
Once you've used your 5 free emails, you can upgrade to any paid plan. Upgrades are instant — plans start at just $6.95/month and can be cancelled anytime with no penalties.
Does it work with Gmail or Outlook?
Yes — our Chrome extension integrates directly into Gmail. For Outlook and other clients, simply copy the generated email with one click and paste it in. Direct Outlook integration is coming soon.
Will the emails sound like they were written by AI?
No — that's our core mission. MailCraft AI adapts to your specified tone, style, and context. The emails read naturally and authentically. Our users regularly report that their recipients can't tell the difference.
